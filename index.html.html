I have over 6 years of experience in the tech industry, specializing in web development and UI/UX design. My journey began with a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites, and over the years, I've honed my skills and expanded my expertise.

As a full stack developer, I am proficient in both front-end and back-end technologies. My skill set includes:

  • Front-End: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular
  • Back-End: Node.js, Express, PHP, Python, Ruby on Rails
  • Database: MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL
  • Other: Git, Docker, AWS, RESTful APIs
  • WordPress: 100% - Expert in building and customizing WordPress sites, including themes and plugins.
  • HTML: 75% - Proficient in writing clean, semantic HTML code.
  • PHP: 85% - Experienced in server-side scripting and creating dynamic web applications.
  • CSS: 99% - Skilled in creating responsive designs and using CSS pre-processors like SASS.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a strong advocate for user-centered design and strive to create interfaces that are both visually appealing and easy to use. My projects range from small business websites to large-scale web applications, and I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow.

My Services

  • Web Development: Building responsive and functional websites using the latest technologies. I specialize in front-end and back-end development to create seamless user experiences.
  • UI/UX Design: Crafting intuitive and aesthetically pleasing user interfaces. I focus on user-centered design principles to ensure the best user experience.
  • Graphic Design: Creating visually appealing graphics for websites, social media, and marketing materials. My design process includes understanding client needs and delivering high-quality visuals.
  • Software Testing: Ensuring the quality and functionality of web applications through rigorous testing processes. I perform both manual and automated testing to identify and fix issues.
  • SEO Optimization: Improving website visibility and search engine rankings through effective SEO strategies. I optimize content, improve site speed, and ensure mobile-friendliness.
  • Content Management: Developing and managing content for websites using CMS platforms like WordPress. I ensure content is well-organized, up-to-date, and engaging.
  • E-commerce Solutions: Building robust e-commerce platforms to help businesses sell online. I integrate payment gateways, design user-friendly shopping experiences, and provide ongoing support.
  • Consulting Services: Offering expert advice on web development, design, and digital strategies. I work closely with clients to understand their goals and provide tailored solutions.
